The best movies according to Daniel

My top 3 movies of all time

Star Wars: A New Hope

Amazing visuals and an awesome story that my 11 year old self loved.

Spider-Man: Across the Spider-verse

The animation, story, characters are all amazing. Probably one of the best animated movies I have ever watched.

Avengers: Endgame

An amazing end to an amazing movie franchise. It invokes a lot of different emotions and I love it a lot.
